Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my package?

You can track your package by entering your tracking number on the UPS tracking page. Tracking numbers are typically provided by the sender and can be found on your shipping confirmation email or receipt.

What should I do if my package is delayed?

Package delays can occur due to various reasons including weather conditions, high shipment volumes, or incorrect addresses. Check the tracking information for updates. If your package is significantly delayed, please contact UPS customer support for further assistance.

How do I change the delivery address for my package?

Address changes can be requested through UPS My Choice (if you're a member) or by contacting UPS customer service. There may be a fee for this service, and changes are not guaranteed if the package is already out for delivery.

What are UPS's business hours for customer service?

UPS customer service is available 24/7 for urgent shipment issues. For general inquiries, phone support is available Monday-Friday 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM EST and Saturday 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM EST.

How do I file a claim for a lost or damaged package?

Claims can be filed online through the UPS website or by contacting customer service. You'll need your tracking number, proof of value for the contents, and photos if the package was damaged. Claims must be filed within 60 days of the shipment date.
